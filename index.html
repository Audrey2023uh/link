Conclusion & Key Findings

Summary of EDS6397 Group 01 multimodal incident analysis

Total Incidents Analyzed

1,252 integrated records from combined_dataset2.csv across five modalities.

Audio Dominant Source

Audio contributed 708 records (56.5%) — the largest modality in the corpus.

Statistical Significance

Chi-square and Kruskal-Wallis tests both returned p < 0.001, confirming significant cross-modality differences.

Sentiment Distribution

Negative 754 · Neutral 101 · Positive 397 (VADER on Summary text).

Severity Distribution

Low 282 · Medium 103 · High 219 · Critical 648. 867 incidents classified High or Critical.

Main Contribution

End-to-end multimodal pipeline with TF-IDF RAG prototype, 25-figure analytics report, severity classification, and decision-support dashboard.
